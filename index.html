Hello, I'm Michael Whittemore.

I'm a Bay Area based software developer with five and half years of experience, focused on web and real time video communication. I'm a full stack generalist. My past experience on a small team required me to wear many hats. I've done everything from API design and implementation, to doubling as our team's SDET (both writing automated tests and manually testing across different browsers and devices), to working on our CI/CD pipeline and performing deployments, to working with our backend architect on piping media to our transcription serve, to working on demos and technical documentation for our DevRel team.

I'm an East Bay native and I currently live in Oakland, CA with my technical PM wife, our two cats, and our ball python. Some of my non-tech hobbies include live music (primarily metal), boardgames (my recent favorites include SETI, Dune: Imperium, and Hasna Teutonica), and classes at my local gym. Most days I can be found working on my personal projects at my local cafe alongside many a glass of cold brewed coffee. I always love the opportunity to talk about music and games.
